Fun places to bring your toddler in the St. Louis Area

Saint louis has many great places to visit from going to the top of the Arch or going to watch a baseball game at busch stadium. But do you have an over active toddler that is adventurous, curious and loves exploring? Here are some great places in St. Louis you can bring them to watch them learn and enjoy themselves!

Saint Louis Zoo

The Saint Louis Zoo is a great place for kids to see all the different species that live with us in this wonderful world. Bring them to enjoy a ride on the railroad around the zoo or experience the sealion show!

Saint Louis Aquarium

The Saint Louis Aquarium is a new attraction located at the Union Station. Featuring over 13000 sea animals, including sharks and stingrays, this will sure to excite your child!

The Magic House

The Magic House contains many exhibits for kids to interact with. Watch them engage in hands-on learning and experiment with many different features.

Grant's Farm

Visit Grant's Farm for the amazing chance to see some of St. Louis history as well as all the different types of animals. They also feature the Anheuser-Busch Clysdesdales.

City Museum

The City Museum is the best childrens playground that features a 10 story slide as well as many tunnels to explore and a rooftop you need to see to believe!

Saint Louis Science Center

The St. Louis Science Center is one of the top 5 science centers in the US! Serving over 1.2 millions visitors annually, come see them explore the wonders of outer space or travel back time to the historic era!

National Museum of Transportation

The National Museum of Transportation has the biggest collection of transportation vehicles in the world! From trains, to cars, to planes and boats! Come see the history of North America's transportation heritage.
